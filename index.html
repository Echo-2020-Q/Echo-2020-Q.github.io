About Me

Hi, I'm Qin Yihang, currently pursuing a master's degree at the Complex Networks and Control Lab. My research focuses on promoting cooperation through artificial intelligence, particularly deep reinforcement learning and evolutionary game theory. My interests include spatial evolutionary game simulations and human-subject behavioral experiments. I hope my research can contribute to the natural sciences in human society! If you feel we share similar interests, feel free to reach out for discussion~
