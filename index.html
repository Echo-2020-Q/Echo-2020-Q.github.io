About Me

I am an M.Sc. student in Automation at Shanghai Jiao Tong University. My research focuses on promoting cooperation through artificial intelligence, particularly deep reinforcement learning and evolutionary game theory. My interests include spatial evolutionary game simulations and human-subject behavioral experiments.
